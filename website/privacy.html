Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Oppli does not collect, store, or share personal data on external servers. All information you enter (companies, jobs, contacts, coffee chats, actions) is stored locally in your browser (e.g., localStorage/IndexedDB).

Data Collection

  • No account, no server-side database.
  • Data remains on your device unless you export it yourself.

Browser Permissions

The Chrome extension (if installed) only accesses the current LinkedIn tab to read visible page content you already see, in order to save items locally.

Analytics

No analytics by default. If we add privacy-preserving analytics later, we will update this page.
